About me

I'm a web designer and front-end developer from Kyiv, Ukraine. I enjoy turning complex problems into simple, beautiful and intuitive interface designs.

My passion is to develop from the ground up, to do so I continuously explore and learn new useful languages, frameworks, libraries, and techniques, which I find out it's also the best way to improve and keep up to date my current skills.
